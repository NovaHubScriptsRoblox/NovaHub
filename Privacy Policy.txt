Effective Date: April 2, 2026

At NovaHUB, we value your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how you can request its removal.

1. Data We Collect
NovaHUB only collects data necessary for its core functions. This includes:

User IDs: To identify users for leveling, settings, or permissions.

Server IDs: To store server-specific configurations and preferences.

Command Usage: We may log which commands are used to help us improve the bot’s performance.

Role/Channel IDs: If configured for moderation or auto-role features.

2. How We Use Data
The data collected is used strictly for:

Providing and maintaining the Bot’s features.

Ensuring server-specific settings (like prefix or log channels) are saved.

Preventing abuse and ensuring the security of our services.

3. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties. Data is only shared if:

Required by law or to comply with a legal process.

Necessary to protect the safety and rights of the NovaHUB developers and users.

4. Third-Party Services
NovaHUB interacts with the Discord API. Please refer to Discord’s Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data. If the Bot interacts with Roblox-related APIs, we do not store your Roblox password or sensitive account credentials.

5. Data Retention & Deletion
Retention: We only store data as long as the Bot is present in your server or as long as you are an active user.

Deletion: You can request that your data be deleted at any time. To do so, please join our support server (Code: P7rCJAW7wN) and contact an administrator.

Automated Deletion: If the Bot is kicked from a server, server-specific data may be deleted from our database after a period of inactivity.

6. Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify users of any significant data breaches via our support server.

7. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Discord:
Support Server: https://discord.gg/P7rCJAW7wN
